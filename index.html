BUCK

BUCK is the future.
The ultimate cryptocurrency.

The zkSNARKs Zero-knowledge proving scheme is a decentralized and open-source technology that offers privacy and selective transparency of transactions.

BUCK provides near instant settlement with the option for unshielded and shielded transations. Transaction fees for most transactions are 0.0001 BUCK. With low transaction fees and its limited 1 billion coin maximum supply, BUCK is the perfect transaction platform for now and the future.

BUCK utilizes the same parameters produced in the Zcash secure trusted setup meeting (source).
